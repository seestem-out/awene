Who are we?

Awene Empire Group Properties is a real estate and Property Developers Company in Namibia, specializing in property rentals, plots sales, leasing and sales residential, commercial and industrial and agricultural. We assist sellers in selling their properties all over Namibia, support buyers in finding their dream homes and help landlords find tenants for their properties. We provide professional services design-build, general contracting, architecture, engineering and construction management and systems integration for projects large and small.
